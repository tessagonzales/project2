Packing up 3/6/08

As I'm packing, I'm trying to figure out if I should bring my green shirt or my red shirt? I feel like green brings out my eyes, but red pops out more as someone could pick me out in the crowd. I mean, what if I get lost? Then if Ryan (pencil-throwing meathead) has to give a description of me. He could just be like, well, he's wearing red, he'll stick out like a sore thumb. In my head that makes sense. So I will pack a week's worth of red shirts. Thinking about Ryan gives me scary flashbacks of his horrible wedgies. And his pencils that he used to throw at me. He would sharpen them and try to attack my legs so he wouldn't be seen by Ms. Frens. I remember that one time he threw so hard that it drew blood. Blood! I didn't think of that! Blood is red, and if for some godforsaken reason I start bleeding my shirt will cover it. And it won't look like I was hurt. Nevermind, forget it. I'll pack the green shirts instead. I would rather avoid it.

The Camera 3/4/08

Did I mention that I'm first class? Oh my gosh, I have never been and I am so excited. Wait till I write it on my blog! Crap, I've actually lost track of time, so I bought a camera. And I'm taking that with me to Cabo. Peace out.

About Me

My therapist says I shouldn't take everything as funny when it hurts, but whatever what does she know? Any-hoo, just going to drop some knowledge... On my blog.
